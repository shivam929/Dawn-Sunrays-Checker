Sunrays Guide

Everything you need to know about sunrays, how to earn them, and how tiers work.

☀️
What are Sunrays?
Sunrays are DAWN's community reputation points. They track your participation and contributions across Sunrise Ceremonies, events, games, quizzes, and community activities. Every sunray you earn reflects your dedication to the DAWN ecosystem.
🎯
How to Earn
Earn sunrays by participating in weekly Sunrise Ceremonies, winning poker tournaments and chess matches, placing in quiz competitions, community MVP recognition, X Signal Sprints, local community events, and various regional activities.
🏆
Ranking System
Your global rank is determined by your total sunrays compared to all other community members. Rankings are updated in real-time as ceremonies occur. Top ranks reflect the most active and dedicated DAWN community participants.
🃏
Tier NFTs
Official tiers (Trailblazer through Dawn Ascendant) are NFT-backed status indicators. Reaching a tier threshold qualifies you for the corresponding NFT. Newcomer is a community tier that doesn't have an NFT but marks your first steps.
